Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

add_circleWhat types of payment does Shop SpaceX accept?

Shop SpaceX accepts all major credit cards and Apple Pay.

add_circleDo you accept exchanges?

No, we are unable to accept direct exchanges.

add_circleCan I return a product purchased from a third party?

Items purchased from a third party must be returned directly to that seller. Unfortunately, SpaceX cannot accept the return of goods purchased from any third-party vendor.

add_circleAm I eligible for a return?

We only accept returns of items that have been delivered within the last 30 days, and are returned in the condition in which they were shipped. This includes, but is not limited to, ensuring items are unused, unworn, have not been stained or washed, and still have the original tags attached.

add_circleHow do I return a product?

For domestic orders, if your return meets the above criteria, please visit https://returns.spacex.com to initiate your return. To cover return shipping and restocking, please note that a flat fee of $8.00 will be deducted from your refund. Refunds will be returned to your original method of payment within 7-10 days of SpaceX receiving your return. If your item does not meet the above criteria upon inspection, you will not receive a refund. If you have an international order or any other questions, please send an e-mail with your order number to merchandise@spacex.com and a SpaceX representative will be in touch.

add_circleHow soon will my order be processed and how will I know when it has been shipped?

You will receive a shipping notice via e-mail when your order has left the warehouse. Orders are fulfilled Monday through Friday. Most orders ship within 2-3 days business days of being placed. Due to constraints from our carriers because of current events, shipments may be delayed.

add_circleHow can I track my order?

When your package has shipped, you will receive a confirmation via e-mail that also contains tracking information from the appropriate carrier.

add_circleThere is a problem with my shipment/my order never arrived - what should I do?

If you experience any problems with your order, please contact customer service at merchandise@spacex.com.
